Welcome

    Welcome to our karate program! Our style is Shito-Ryu. It is a traditional Japanese style that comes to us from the Okinawa Islands. In the “Shito Ryu Martial Arts system you will learn karate, jujitsu, self defense, tournament fighting and street self defense & fighting. Through our training you can gain a high degree of physical fitness, self defense, respect and confidence. We are not boastful, we do not brag but carry with us “a quiet confidence”. What you gain from this system will depend on you. On how much you are willing to train and your dedication to the program.
All students are expected to maintain good character and good grades in school. Your conduct is extremely important. We want you to have fun as well as work hard during class time. Don’t waste our time and your time & money by goofing around and disrupting classes.
Respect for the Dojo, Sensei and other students are a must!! If there are ever any problems or concerns you may have, please come and visit with me.
