Karate My Hobby

This project will be a public bulletin board of the events and practices of karate, more specificlly of the dojo I am apart of. As well, be a basic guide for those individuals wanting to earn a black belt in karate. Like most sites there will be a history page, as well as images of what we do, definitions of key words, and a calander which will inform students, parents, and potentioal clients of when class are held and the topics covered in each of those classes.

The five pages would be:

  • Home page: Will contain a calender of what will be covered at the dojo's practices each month. and will have a json for the special events.
  • Images: Posted images or picture of special events, of student advances in karate, or of the class during practice.
  • Dictionary: Will be a list of key terms and phrases used during class, or which are important to know for other various reason. All this information will come from a json.
  • Katas: This page will have a number of various katas required for advancing in belt rank.
  • History: This page will contain the history of the dojo, as well as the history of our particlar style of karate.

Audience

Primary audience is adults ages 25-40. Most will be parents. Access to the site will be split between mobile devices and laptops.

Audience Goals:

To find events and times easily and effectively as well as help people understand words used frequently as well as the history and importance of the style.
